Copyright

Copyright Searches

Copyright is a form of intellectual property protection provided by the Indian Law, protection is available for original works of authorship that are in a tangible form, published or unpublished, and it includes paintings, literary works, live performances, photographs, movies, and software.

A Copyright Owner's Rights
The Copyright Act gives the copyright owner certain exclusive rights, including the right to:
• Reproduce the work
• Prepare "derivative works" (other works based on the original work)
• Distribute copies of the work by sale, lease, or other transfer of ownership
• Perform the work publicly
• Display the work publicly
The copyright owner also has the right to authorize other people, i.e., transfer his or her exclusive rights -- or any subdivision of those rights -- to others as well.

Our Services in relation to copyright protection include:
• Filing of Copyright Applications with the Copyright Office for registration.
• Responding to Copyright Objection
• Enabling Copyright Registration of paintings, literary works, live performances, photographs, movies, software, Sound Recordings and musical compositions
• Registration of Logo Copyrights for Goods and Services

Details Required:
• Title of the work to be filed
• Name, Address of the applicant (Company name as registered and registration details)
• Name of all directors of the company
• Language of the work
• Date, Month & Year of Publication in India
• Details of authorized signatory
• Name of the author if the author is different than the applicant
