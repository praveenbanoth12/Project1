Trademark Registration

Why Trademark Registration is required:
A. Trademark identifies a company to a customer and trust regarding the company for their product/services offered.
B. Trademark is an intangible asset for a business which is used to protect the company’s investment in the brand or symbol.

Details Required:
• Trade Mark/ Logo you wish to register.
• Name, Address, Nationality, and Particulars of the Applicant (whether Individual, Society, Trust, Partnership firm, or Company).
• Date of first use of the Trade Mark or confirmation that the mark is proposed to be used
• List of goods or services for which registration is required.
• Details of correspondence like name, phone number and email id.

Scope of work we cover under Trade Mark Registration:
